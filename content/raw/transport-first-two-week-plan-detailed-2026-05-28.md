# Transport Programme – First Two Weeks Plan

## Key Anchors

### 1. Enterprise Transport Data Product
Map:
- Core and adjacent data domains
- Source systems
- Ownership
- Access paths
- Reusable asset patterns

### 2. RAMC Bid
Develop:
- Risk-based road maintenance narrative
- Bid proof points
- Initial operational decision use case

### 3. Sensing & Telemetry
Integrate:
- Retina Visions
- BYD telemetry
- Drainage IoT
- Weather data
- Traffic data
- Open road datasets

### 4. Governance & Operating Model
Define:
- Working groups
- Decision rights
- Access blockers
- Meeting evidence
- Handoff to weeks 3–6

## Two-Week Timeline

### Week 1
- Sponsor alignment and scope
- Stakeholder interviews
- Current-state systems and data mapping

### Week 2
- Sensing and RAMC deep dive
- Validation and access blocker resolution
- POC selection and roadmap definition

### Decision Gate
Agree:
- Proof point
- Owner
- Data access
- Week 3 commencement conditions
