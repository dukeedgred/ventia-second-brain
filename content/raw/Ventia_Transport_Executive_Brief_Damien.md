# Integrated Transport Data Asset
**Executive briefing for Damien · Transport**

## Purpose of this Engagement
Ventia's Transport division mobilises people, vehicles, and equipment across hundreds of sites every day. Data currently sits in contract-by-contract silos. This six‑week programme with Omnia Collective / EdgeRed aims to build an integrated transport data asset connecting GPS, telematics, fleet, and job scheduling data into a single usable foundation.

## Outcomes Expected After 6 Weeks

### 1. North Star for the Transport Data Asset
Clear view of how an integrated transport dataset can add value:
1. Winning bids
2. Mobilising workforce/equipment
3. Delivery optimisation/support
4. Additional value via data & insights (e.g. benchmarking costs)

### 2. Status / Progress Map
Visibility of:
- Data available across Transport contracts
- What has been centralised into Databricks
- What remains decentralised and why
- Current usage patterns

### 3. Working Data Foundation
Expansion of centralised transport data assets including:
- Asset management
- GPS
- Telematics
- Fleet management
- Job scheduling
- Vehicle and service locations
- Contract KPIs
- Service provision information

### 4. Live Use Case
A business-tested use case delivering immediate value in:
- Bid support
- Service mobilisation/readiness
- Contract operations and reporting
- Benchmarking and insights

### 5. Delivery Roadmap
Roadmap covering:
- Future priorities
- Timeline and effort estimates
- Operational changes required
- Investment recommendations

## Note
This data asset will continue growing beyond the six‑week programme. The goal is to establish foundations and provide confidence for future investment decisions.
