# Transport Gen 3 Tender Innovation

Transport Gen 3 tender innovation is the bid-driven push to demonstrate credible AI, technology uplift, autonomous defect and hazard recognition, and visual decision-support capabilities for TMR. The source frames this as both a near-term tender differentiator and a longer-term roadmap connected to [[Transport Asset Intelligence Roadmap]].

## Tender Context

The embedded Evolve/RAMC discussion says TMR's documents may not explicitly require AI or technology uplift, but there is an underlying expectation for innovation. Claire and Liz described a need to show tangible current capability plus a credible future roadmap, while avoiding overpromising untested technology.

The group expected competitors to promise advanced features, so the response needs evidence of deliverable solutions, a clear implementation timeline, and a balance of automation with human oversight.

## Demo Strategy

Sanja proposed a demo integrating SAP, IBM, and Nextspace, potentially using real data and vendor pre-sales support. The team discussed a tight timeline: a demonstrable solution by mid-June, with further refinement before the July tender submission.

The demo needs a clear use case and story. Claire and Rhys were identified as owners for drafting the scenario and gathering data, while Sanja, Katrina, Wayne, and Chris were tied to demo coordination. The transcript also raises probity concerns about how much should be shown before or after submission.

## Asset Tooling And Transition

The tender discussion compared [[Asset Vision]], SAP, Nextspace, DTIMS, and the new Advice tool being piloted by TMR. [[Asset Vision]] is expected to be renewed for at least two more years to preserve continuity, while SAP and Nextspace are considered as possible future-state components.

The source also records concerns about cost, innovation, data ownership, change management, and operational adoption. This makes the tender roadmap partly a technology selection problem and partly an operating-model and trust problem.

## Future Roadmap Themes

Future themes include IoT-enabled predictive maintenance, crowdsourced maintenance tasks, AI-driven decision support, weather-event prediction, and proactive network management. These themes connect the bid narrative to [[Ventia AI and Innovation Portfolio]] and [[Ventia Data Platform Modernisation]].
