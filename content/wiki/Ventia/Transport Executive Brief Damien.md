# Transport Executive Brief Damien

This source is an executive briefing for Damien on the proposed [[Integrated Transport Data Asset]] programme for Ventia Transport. It is a short briefing note rather than meeting minutes, and it summarises the purpose, six-week outcomes, candidate use cases, and roadmap expectations for the engagement.

## Summary

The brief says Ventia Transport mobilises people, vehicles, and equipment across hundreds of sites daily, while relevant data remains split across contract-specific silos. The six-week programme with Omnia Collective / EdgeRed is intended to connect GPS, telematics, fleet, and job scheduling data into a single usable foundation.

The work is expected to produce a north star, status map, working data foundation, live business-tested use case, and delivery roadmap. The brief emphasises that the asset will keep growing beyond the six-week period; the immediate goal is to establish foundations and give Ventia confidence for future investment decisions.

## Key Details

- The north star should show how an integrated Transport dataset can support bid wins, mobilisation, delivery optimisation, and value-added insights such as cost benchmarking.
- The status map should identify data available across Transport contracts, what has been centralised into Databricks, what remains decentralised and why, and current usage patterns.
- The working foundation should expand centralised data assets across asset management, GPS, telematics, fleet management, job scheduling, vehicle and service locations, contract KPIs, and service provision information.
- The live use case should deliver immediate value in bid support, service mobilisation/readiness, contract operations and reporting, or benchmarking and insights.
- The roadmap should cover future priorities, timeline and effort estimates, operational changes, and investment recommendations.
