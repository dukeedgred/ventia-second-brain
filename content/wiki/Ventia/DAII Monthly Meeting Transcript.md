# DAII Monthly Meeting Transcript

## Summary

This source is a transcript of a Data, AI and Innovation monthly meeting. It covers updates on [[Ventia Data Governance Framework]], the [[Safety Metrics Governance Pilot]], Investor Day showcases, [[EDW Ernie]], [[Ventia Data Platform Modernisation]], and the broader [[Ventia AI and Innovation Portfolio]].

The meeting format combined program updates, video showcases, a live AI-agent demo, around-the-grounds updates from data engineering and innovation, and team reflections from people working across data governance, cybersecurity, engineering, and innovation.

## Key Details

- Data governance is moving from framework design into execution, with safety metrics chosen as the priority pilot because they are regulated, already have a Databricks model that can be governed, and sit high in enterprise and operational risk.
- The governance team is cataloguing critical data elements and business context in Alation, including exploration of Alation CD Manager to reduce manual CDE identification.
- The target governance pattern is "controls by design": governance, definitions, ownership, traceability, quality controls, and trusted delivery embedded into data products as they are built.
- Sustainability is an adjacent governance candidate: the business team has strong business-process controls, but current data pipelines are manual and may need automation before mature data products are created.
- Investor Day included a DAII team video using Ventia's refreshed brand and sizzle reels for VenIQ and AI Apprentice.
- EDW Ernie was demonstrated as an AI agent that can review warehouse views, discover reusable Databricks assets, draft SQL aligned to engineering standards, and support BW replatforming.
- Data engineering updates covered Program Evolve, S/4HANA data ingestion into Databricks, SIT1/SIT2 progress, New Zealand Transport work management reporting, ACMA vendor rank/rating work, and dependency on IBM delivery.
- Innovation updates covered the Apple Watch safety trial, Beklar safety app, VenSpark campaign momentum, and SWIMS AI.
