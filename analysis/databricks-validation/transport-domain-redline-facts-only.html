High-level domains for the Transport data asset

Red text is factual correction or validation status. Red strikethrough is wording not supported by the current table documentation or the Databricks source-table validation run.

1. Contract & Network context (the spine everything hangs off)

  • Contract (source-validated examples: WRU, SRAP-C, TSRC, RAMC Gen2, Brisbane Airport, Port of Brisbane, SHT/WHT, Auckland West Transport, VentureSmart; VRMC, NEL and City Harbour were not found in the validated Asset Vision source catalogues), business unit, client, operational status/stage fields where present Bid -> Mobilise -> Plan -> Inspect -> Renew -> Report was not validated as a source-table lifecycle structure, contract type (roads vs tunnel), commercial model as a source-table-validated driver of item-level data was not found.
  • Network / route / segment / linear referencing: source tables contain asset hierarchy, WKT/spatial information and chainage fields where populated.

2. Asset register & hierarchy (your "hierarchy of assets and their properties")

  • Parent asset: parent asset hierarchy is source-backed; Road or Tunnel as the universal parent asset was not validated.
  • Child asset categories: pavements, drainage (pits, lines), vehicle barriers/guardrails, signage, bridges, culverts, lighting, jet fans (tunnels), etc. Asset categories vary by contract/catalogue.
  • Per-asset attributes: ID, type, sub-type, location/geometry, dimensions, material, install date, status, parent asset, WKT/spatial information and chainage where populated. Ownership categories of ours vs disputed vs client's were not validated. Handover/practical completion and as-built mismatch references were found in source forms. AI-detected as asset provenance was not validated; AI Defect ID was found in defect/job-related source data.
  • Asset condition: condition fields exist, but population varies by contract/catalogue. A globally populated 1-5 condition scale was not validated.

3. Inspections (separate from defects)

  • Scheduled inspection runs: inspection tables/views contain scheduled and completed dates where populated; coverage varies by contract/catalogue.
  • Inspection record: who, when, what route covered, method (inspection/form/tunnel maintenance or event records are present in source tables), evidence (imagery, photos, video, GPS trail where populated). RetinaVision, AID camera inspections and dash-cam capture intervals were not validated in the source tables.
  • Link to assets observed and condition assessments made: inspection records include asset links, job links, capital-work links and WKT where populated; coverage varies by contract/catalogue.

4. Defects & Hazards (one model, severity-flagged)

  • Raised from: inspection, public report, incidental observation where captured in source forms/fields. AID alert as a validated source-table trigger was not found.
  • Attributes: asset link, defect type / hazard-defect code, severity/hazard level where populated, photo evidence, raised by, raised at, location/WKT.
  • SLA / response time: due date and compliance fields are source-backed. The source tables did not validate the exact SLA auto-calculation rule based on severity plus asset category.

5. Jobs / Work orders (the response side)

  • Triggered by defect/hazard OR scheduled maintenance: job, vjob, jobasset and workflow/status tables are source-backed. Capital-work links exist where populated. Capital works proposal as a universal direct trigger was not validated.
  • Crew/resource assignment, scheduled date, response SLA/due date, completion timestamp, before/after/during photos, completion status: these are source-backed where populated.
  • Routine maintenance vs Capital Works distinction: capitalwork and vcapitalwork tables exist. The rule "anything routine can't fix -> capital works" was not validated in the source tables.

6. Sensing & telemetry (your "sensing side")

  • Source forms contain camera/CCTV terms, GPS/location fields, tunnel equipment alarm/check fields, OMCS references, fan-related maintenance fields and AI Defect ID.
  • Asset Vision dash cam every 5-10m was not validated. RetinaVision every 3m was not validated. AID cameras for tunnel incident detection were not validated. Fleet telematics were not validated.
  • Sensor -> event -> incident/work order flow: source-table lineage for this full flow was not validated.

7. KPIs & Reporting

  • Contract KPI definitions: measurement fields exist for inspections, jobs, due dates, compliance, completion, workflow/status and asset condition where populated. A single global KPI definition schema was not validated.
  • KPI measurements: inspections scheduled vs completed, jobs raised vs closed within SLA/compliance, asset condition distribution where condition is populated, pavement performance where present in contract-specific forms/fields.
  • Monthly contract report outputs: 50+ page Word report outputs were not validated from the source tables.

8. Incidents (tunnel-specific, distinct from defects)

  • Tunnel event/incident handling appears in source forms and OMCS-related wording. A complete OMCS incident-management table/feed was not validated in the Asset Vision source tables.
  • The source validation found Motorway Event Log and OMCS-related form fields.

A few things I'd flag before locking this in

  • Contract is the partitioning key: validated. Shared core source tables and contract-specific form/module fields are present. Mandatory-field standardisation across contracts was not validated from the source tables.
  • Roads vs Tunnels diverge meaningfully in the sensing layer: tunnel-specific camera/CCTV, fan, alarm, OMCS and Motorway Event Log terms were found. Always-on AID plus OMCS alarm flow was not validated as source-table lineage.
  • As-built / handover data is its own beast: handover/practical completion and as-built mismatch fields were found. PDFs plus asset registers plus GeoJSON at handover were not validated from the source tables.
  • Capital Works / Forward Programme: capitalwork and vcapitalwork tables are present. dTIMS fields are present in source forms. A direct dTIMS system feed was not validated.
